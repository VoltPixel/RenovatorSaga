Privacy Policy

App: Renovator Saga  |  Developer: VoltPixel
Last updated: March 28, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how VoltPixel ("we", "us", "our") handles information in connection with the mobile game Renovator Saga (the "App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect or store any personally identifiable information (name, email address, phone number, etc.) directly.

The following categories of non-personal data may be collected automatically through third-party services integrated into the App:

  • Device information – device model, operating system version, language setting.
  • Gameplay events – anonymised events such as level start/complete/fail, power-up usage, and in-app purchase attempts. These events are used solely for improving game balance and user experience.
  • Advertising identifiers – an anonymised advertising ID (GAID on Android) may be used to serve relevant advertisements, only if you grant consent through the in-app GDPR consent screen.

2. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services that may collect data according to their own privacy policies:

  • Unity Ads (Unity Technologies) – displays rewarded video and banner advertisements.
    Privacy policy: unity.com/legal/privacy-policy
  • Unity Analytics (Unity Technologies) – collects anonymised gameplay telemetry.
    Privacy policy: unity.com/legal/privacy-policy
  • Google Play Billing (Google LLC) – processes in-app purchases. We do not receive or store your payment information.
    Privacy policy: policies.google.com/privacy

3. Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The App saves game progress (level reached, coins, completed rooms, settings) exclusively on your device using Unity PlayerPrefs. This data never leaves your device and is deleted when you uninstall the App.

4. Advertising and Your Consent (GDPR)

On first launch you will see a consent screen. You may choose to:

  • Opt in or out of personalised advertisements.
  • Opt in or out of analytics data collection.

If you decline personalised ads, you may still see non-personalised advertisements. You can change your choices at any time in the in-game Settings screen.

5. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

6. Data Retention

Anonymised analytics data retained by Unity is subject to Unity's own retention policies. Local device data is retained until the App is uninstalled.
