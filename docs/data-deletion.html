What data does the app store?

Renovator Saga does not require an account and does not collect any personally identifiable information (name, email, phone number, etc.).

All game progress (levels, stars, decorations, settings) is stored locally on your device only using Unity PlayerPrefs. This data is not transmitted to our servers.

How to delete local game data

To delete all locally stored game data, simply uninstall the app from your device. This will permanently remove all game progress and settings.

On Android: Settings → Apps → Renovator Saga → Uninstall

Alternatively, you can clear app data without uninstalling: Settings → Apps → Renovator Saga → Storage → Clear Data

Third-party analytics data

The app uses Firebase Analytics and Unity Ads, which may collect anonymised usage data (gameplay events, device identifiers). This data is not personally identifiable.

You can disable analytics and personalised ads at any time via the GDPR consent screen in the app (Settings → Privacy).

To request deletion of any anonymised analytics data associated with your device, please contact us at the email below.

To request data deletion, contact us at:

Please include "Data Deletion Request - Renovator Saga" in the subject line. We will process your request within 30 days.

What data will be deleted

  • Local data – deleted immediately when you uninstall the app or clear app data.
  • Firebase Analytics data – anonymised event data will be deleted from Google servers upon request (within 30 days).
  • Unity Ads data – advertising identifier data will be reset when you reset your device advertising ID.
